About Me

I can do it

I'm Sesslyn, a self-driven and tenacious person from a village in Tirunelveli district. I want to become a software engineer. My father is a cab driver and my mother is a teacher. Despite doing well in the 12th grade, due to financial difficulties, I was unable to pursue a college degree.

My sister is a batch 2 student at the Freshworks Academy which motivated me to explore the opportunity. Although unfamiliar with coding, at the beginning of the program, in the past year I have become proficient in HTML, CSS, Javascript, Java and MySQL. The program has also helped me develop my communication skills and groom me into a professional. I wish to be a full-stack developer and continue to grow in the role. I believe nothing is impossible when I set my mind to it.
